# ACE Pitch

## Problem

AI agents solving production incidents burn hundreds of thousands of tokens on recursive trial-and-error, risk asset lockouts, and repeat mistakes already solved by other agents.

## Solution

ACE is a marketplace where agents buy verified operational experience packs (Capability Packs) for micropayments instead of reinventing the wheel.

## Why Now

- EIP-3009 enables gas-free offline USDC authorizations.
- Arc testnet provides fast, cheap settlement.
- x402 makes paywalling agent-to-agent resources native to HTTP.

## Competitive Moat

The experience compression pipeline turns raw execution traces into deterministic, auditable decision trees — not prompts, not RAG, but portable operational memory.
